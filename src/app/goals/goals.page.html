Goals
WeekList - Info WeekList is a shopping list app. Usually similar applications let the user to type the items. This application will be using a local database and smart algorithms to remember things that user buys more often and also trying to predict when items that purchased periodically will need to be purchased again.
  • Using the app the user will have the ability to add multiple lists for different stores as well as future lists.
  • The app will store different stores, depending on the type.
  • It will also have a weekly plan for shopping in the next months.
  • Users can create accounts and share his lists with another account.
  • Users can also add prices and group items in categories.
  • WeekList will also take advantage of QR reader, to read barcodes and store items.
